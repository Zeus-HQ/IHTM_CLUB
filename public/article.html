    "Social Media Project to Promote Tourism"

    In 2012, almost after 3 months of joining theRegional Office for KSA/GCC Department ofCulture & Tourism - Abu Dhabi. I discussed withmy boss the idea of collaborating with a saudicompany to produce a social media project. The idea was to showcase Abu Dhabi as a tourismdestination to the KSA/GCC market, and he was very welcoming of the idea in general!

    {Takki} The project

    I did my market research and the best option was U-Turn Company. I presented it to my boss and he liked the company and their work. Therefore, I called them and scheduled a meeting and went there to meet Mr. Omar Hussain (the CEO at that time), we agreed to work on Takki as our project. The deal was made to film two episodes of the series in Abu Dhabi!

    •• Project success!

    After working on the project for more than 8 months, It started to see the light of success. the filming permits were granted and the shooting was scheduled to take place on March 2013! Takki filmed episodes number 11 and 12 in AbuDhabi with more than 6.7 million views for both episodes in total.

    “Never dream of success, work for it!”
